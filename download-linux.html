Requirements

You will need to get the GLib, Pango and GTK+ packages to build GTK+.
You may also need some of the external dependencies that are also linked for each version listed below.

Stable Release

The build guide for GTK+ should cover the basic steps. For any other issues, see the FAQ. Before upgrading from an earlier version of GTK+, you may wish to review the migration guide.

  • GTK+ 3.0
  • GLib 2.28
  • Gdk-Pixbuf 2.22
  • Pango 1.28

Older Versions

You can have the run-time and development environments for GTK+ 3.x, GTK+ 2.x and GTK+ 1.2 installed simultaneously on your computer.

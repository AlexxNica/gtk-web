Submit GTK+ News

The news listed on the news page is provided to us by freshmeat.net. We do not have a good way to provide news which is not on freshmeat.

What that means is that if you want application news to show up here, go add it to freshmeat via the freshmeat contribution page. Then e-mail newapp@gtk.org once the application news shows up at freshmeat and we will set a flag so the app is shown on our page.

We get freshmeat news slightly after it appears on freshmeat, but it should be pretty quick most the time. (Within 24 hours definately.)

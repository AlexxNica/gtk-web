GTK-Doc is mainly used to document the GTK+ and GNOME libraries. The documentation for these is available online:

  • GLib
  • GObject
  • Pango
  • GdkPixbuf
  • GDK
  • GTK+

The complete list of GTK+ and GNOME online documentation can be found on the GNOME developers website.
